WATER

The Waterfall Lifecycle involves 6 steps, of which all follow a linear pattern. An old, but trialed method that has been used for years. Some merits are:

  • A simple model to interpret
  • Easy judgement of progress
    • Some Disadvantages:
    • Lot's of documentation!!!
    • As the model is linear, if the requirements are not met the process bag steps a lot, which can be costly and time consuming
    • Users are not involved in the process until testing, which can affect the point above
    • Huge delay between start of the project and some prototyped code
